# Proteomic insight <i>Leucoptera coffeella</I>

 Proteomic Insights into Larval Stages of Coffee Leaf Miner (<I>Leucoptera coffeella</I>): A Step Towards the Development of Pest Management strategies


PACBio long-read and paired-end Illumina DNA sequencing were performed from pupae samples, yielding more than 436 Gb of subreads and 31 million reads, with an N50 read length of 15,512 nt, an average read length of 13.8 kb, and a maximum read length of 420.7 kb. Additionally, 20 Gb of short-read DNA sequencing data were used in the assembly, producing 1,984 contigs with a total length of 397 Mb. From these contigs, 39,930 coding DNA sequences (CDSs) were predicted using BRAKER2. The following files correspond to the predicted protein and coding sequence datasets.
